About Web Development

Front-End

Front-end development is an essential part. It dominates your user's perception regarding your website and for what purpose they use your site. Generally, front-end development is the incorporation of JavaScript, CSS, HTML, etc.

Back-End

Back-End Developers work on behind-the-scenes systems and structures that are not visible for users but. Specialists in back-end development tend to be skilled at problem-solving and logic and work with a variety of programming languages such as Python, Ruby, and SQL.

Full Stack

A Full Stack Developer is someone who works with the Back End or server-side of the application as well as the Front End, or client side. Full Stack Developers have to have some skills in a wide variety of coding niches, from databases to graphic design in order to do their job well.
